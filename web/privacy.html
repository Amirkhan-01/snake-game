Privacy Policy

Snake — Premium Edition · Last updated: 2026

Short version: This is a browser game. It does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. Everything you do stays on your device.

What we store

The game saves your progress — high score, coins, unlocked skins, settings, statistics, achievements and leaderboard — in your browser's localStorage. This data lives only on your device and is never uploaded to any server. Clearing your browser data, or using the in-game “Reset progress” button, removes it permanently.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not create accounts or ask for personal information.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking cookies.
  • We do not send your data to us or any third party.

Third-party services

The base game requires no external services and works fully offline. If a distributed build enables optional advertising or in-app purchases, those features are provided by their respective platforms (for example Google AdMob or an app store), which have their own privacy policies. The version you are reading this from does not include active advertising or data collection.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out to the developer through the store or site where you downloaded the game.
