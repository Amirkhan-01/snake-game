    How to play

    Guide the snake to eat food and grow. Chain quick bites for a combo multiplier. Grab power-ups: 🛡 shield, 2× score, 🧲 magnet. Don't hit yourself, walls (except Classic) or obstacles. Move with swipe, arrow keys or WASD.

    Privacy

    This game runs entirely in your browser. Your progress is saved locally on your device and never sent anywhere. Privacy ↗ · Terms ↗
